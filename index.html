Roma Mia

Nestled in the vibrant neighborhood of Clerkenwell, London, Roma Mia brings the warmth, flavors, and traditions of Italy straight to your table. Clerkenwell, renowned for its rich culture and Italian roots, provides the perfect backdrop for a dining experience that feels like a journey to the heart of Rome.

Our beautifully spacious restaurant is designed to feel both intimate and inviting, with elegant interiors that reflect the charm of an authentic Italian trattoria. Whether you’re enjoying a romantic dinner for two or gathering with friends and family, the ambiance at Roma Mia strikes the perfect balance between comfort and sophistication.

At the core of Roma Mia is our passionate team of talented Italian chefs and staff, dedicated to delivering an unforgettable dining experience. Our menu celebrates the best of classic Italian cuisine. Every plate is crafted with the finest ingredients, sourced for their exceptional quality and freshness, ensuring that every bite is a tribute to Italy’s culinary heritage.

Roma Mia is more than just a restaurant; it’s an experience, a place where flavors come alive, traditions are honored, and moments are shared.
Enjoy a bottle of wine, savor authentic cuisine, and let the magic of Clerkenwell and the spirit of Italy make your night unforgettable.
