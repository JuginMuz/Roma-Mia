Roman food is the soul of the Eternal City, served with bold flavors and timeless charm

SCROLL

Our journey began in 1992, in the heart of London, inspired by the rich culinary traditions of Rome and the diverse flavors of Italy.
From the very start, our mission has been to bring an authentic taste of Italian culture to the vibrant streets of this bustling city.
From our handmade pasta to wood-fired pizzas, every dish is crafted with love, using age-old recipes and the finest ingredients sourced from both local markets and trusted Italian producers.
Over the years, we’ve become a gathering place for food lovers, where the rich heritage of Italian cuisine meets the cosmopolitan energy of London.

We are committed to sustainability, supporting local farmers, and working with trusted Italian artisans to bring the best of both worlds to your plate.
Our menu reflects the essence of Italian culture—bold, vibrant, and rooted in family values.
That’s why every dish we serve is crafted with passion, using authentic techniques and the freshest, highest-quality ingredients.
Whether it’s your first visit or your hundredth, we are dedicated to making you feel at home, sharing not just our food, but our love for the culinary art that defines who we are.

Meet our Head Chef, Maria

For over a decade, Chef Maria has been the heart and soul of our kitchen.
Growing up in a family where food was love, Maria mastered the art of simple, authentic flavors that make Italian cooking so unforgettable.
Her favorite dish? The classic Spaghetti Carbonara.
Maria believes this dish embodies the essence of Roman cooking—bold, honest, and irresistibly delicious.

Meet Our Founder, Paola

Born and raised in Trastevere, the vibrant heart of Rome, Paola grew up surrounded by the rich traditions and flavors of Roman cuisine.
With a dream of sharing her hometown’s culinary magic, she brought the taste of Rome to London, creating a space where authenticity and passion come together.
Paola is more than just the founder—she’s the soul of our restaurant.
A warm and lovely presence, she still lends her expert touch in the kitchen, ensuring every dish carries the spirit of her beloved Trastevere.
